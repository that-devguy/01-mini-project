About Us

Welcome to Boba Haven, your sanctuary for delicious boba tea in the heart of LA! We pride ourselves on using only the freshest ingredients and unique flavors to craft the perfect cup of boba. Stop by and try our signature drinks, relax in our cozy atmosphere, and take a break from the hustle and bustle of the city. At Boba Haven, every sip is a haven for your taste buds. See you soon!

caption

  • "The Boba-licious Blast" - A fruity and refreshing blend of strawberry, peach, and mango with chewy boba pearls.
  • "The Boba-nator" - A classic milk tea with a twist, made with rich and creamy vanilla ice cream, topped with boba pearls and a drizzle of caramel.
  • "Rainbow Rush" - A colorful and fun drink made with a rainbow of flavors including strawberry, blueberry, green apple and kiwi, finished with a generous helping of boba pearls.
  • "The Boba-ccino" - A perfect blend of coffee and tea, this drink is made with rich espresso and a hint of caramel, topped with boba pearls and a sprinkle of cinnamon.
  • "Boba-Island Adventure" - A tropical paradise in a cup, made with coconut milk, pineapple juice, and a hint of ginger, finished with a generous helping of boba pearls.
